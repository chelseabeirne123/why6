Let's Go Deeper

Your initial "why" is just the beginning. The real power lies beneath the surface. Let's excavate the deeper truth that will sustain you when motivation fades and obstacles arise.

Reflect on these questions:

• What would achieving this goal prove about who you are as a person?
• What pain or frustration would remain unresolved if you don't pursue this?
• How would your life feel different - not just look different - if you succeeded?
• What example would you be setting, and for whom?
